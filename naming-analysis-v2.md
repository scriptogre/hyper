## Why These Work Better

1. **Search Dominance**: Searching "Weftly" or "Pylx" will immediately find your framework, not millions of unrelated results
2. **Trademark Potential**: Invented words are much easier to trademark
3. **Domain Availability**: Very high likelihood of getting `.com`, `.dev`, `.io`
4. **PyPI Availability**: Nearly guaranteed to be available
5. **Brand Identity**: Unique words create stronger brand recognition
6. **Community**: Easier to create hashtags, communities (e.g., #weftly, #pylx)
